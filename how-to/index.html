How to Order Your Red Panda

Follow these simple steps to bring a cute red panda friend to your doorstep!

Step 1: Choose Your Panda

Visit our Red Panda catalog and select the friend you'd like to meet.

Step 2: Set Your Delivery Preferences

Fill in your delivery details and choose a suitable time for your panda to arrive.

Step 3: Make the Payment

Proceed to checkout and make the payment using our secure system.

Step 4: Track Your Delivery

Once the order is confirmed, you'll receive a tracking number to monitor your panda's journey.

Step 5: Prepare for Arrival

Get your home ready for your new furry friend. Make sure you have plenty of bamboo and a cozy space!
